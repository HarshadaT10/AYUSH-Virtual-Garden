Unani Medicine: A Timeless Healing Tradition

What is Unani Medicine?

Unani medicine is a traditional system of healing and health maintenance that originated in ancient Greece and was later developed in the Arab world. The system is based on the concept of balancing the four essential humors (blood, phlegm, yellow bile, and black bile) to maintain health and well-being.

Health Benefits of Unani Medicine

Unani medicine offers several potential health benefits:

  • Improved digestion and metabolism
  • Enhanced immunity and resistance to diseases
  • Reduction of stress and anxiety
  • Natural detoxification
  • Support for chronic conditions like arthritis and asthma

History & Practices of Unani Medicine

Unani medicine traces its roots back to Greek and Roman times, with Hippocrates and Galen making significant contributions to its understanding. Later, Islamic scholars such as Avicenna (Ibn Sina) refined and popularized the system. The Unani system focuses on the balance of the body's humors and natural therapies to restore harmony.
