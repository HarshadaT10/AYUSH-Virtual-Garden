Ayurveda, meaning "Science of Life", is an ancient Indian holistic healing system that emphasizes balance between the body, mind, and spirit. It focuses on the root cause of ailments and offers natural solutions through lifestyle changes, diet, herbs, yoga, and meditation.

The Five Elements and Doshas

Ayurveda is based on five elements: Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Space. These combine to form three Doshas - Vata, Pitta, and Kapha - that govern body functions.

Vata

Movement, creativity, dryness.
Imbalances: anxiety, dry skin.

Pitta

Metabolism, digestion, heat.
Imbalances: anger, inflammation.

Kapha

Structure, lubrication, stability.
Imbalances: lethargy, congestion.
